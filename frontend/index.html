Portal User Guide & Instructions

Complete guide to generating audit-compliant Bihar Teacher Arrear Forms

🚀 1. Prerequisites (Documents Needed)

  • Latest Pay Slip PDF: Auto-extracts Name, PRAN, PAN, Bank Account, IFSC, and Date of Joining (DOJ).
  • Yearly Payment Statement PDFs: Auto-extracts monthly drawn salary. Supports full 12-month or ongoing/partial FY statements.

📑 2. Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Arrear Scope: Select From & To months. Upload slots adjust automatically.
  2. Upload PDFs: Drag & drop or browse your HRMS statements and pay slip.
  3. School Details: Fill School Name & Block Name.
  4. Designation & Basic Fallbacks: If payslip fails to auto-detect designation, select it manually. If joining month was partial or non-standard, enter your Joining Basic Pay (₹) to lock in the +3% annual increment rule.
  5. Rates & Presets: Choose HRA presets (4%→5%, 7.5%→10%, or Corrected Joiner) and DA rate schedule.

🧮 3. Excel Formulas & Arrear Deduction

The generated Excel embeds live formulas matching official DPO formats:

  • Admissible Net: Gross - NPS - GIS - Professional Tax
  • Drawn Net: Regular Drawn Gross - NPS - GIS
  • Arrear Row: If an arrear bill was drawn in a month, an Arrear Row is added right below that month. Arrear Gross is placed under Basic, Arrear NPS under NPS, and Arrear Net under Net Amount.
  • Net Difference: Automatically subtracts Arrear Row Net from total arrear payable.

🛠️ 4. Manual Verification & Troubleshooting

Value Discrepancy Tip: If any month in the generated Excel has a discrepancy compared to your official records, verify that month against your HRMS Yearly Payment Statement. You can directly paste or type the correct drawn value into the Excel sheet — all formulas will instantly recalculate the totals automatically!
